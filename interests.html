My Passions

The things that keep me curious and motivated every day

Life is more than just school and grades — it's about the experiences, curiosity, and passions that shape who we are. Here are the things I love, explore, and spend my free time on.

⭐ My #1 Passion

Table Tennis

Table tennis is where I truly shine. I have developed a strong skill in the sport over the years — even my teacher Pradeep Sir recognises my ability at the table! I love the speed, reflexes, and strategy involved. It keeps me sharp both physically and mentally, and is my favourite way to unwind after a long day of studying.

🏓
🏙️

City Roaming

I love roaming around Kathmandu, discovering hidden lanes, local food spots, and experiencing the city from a fresh perspective every time I step outside. Every corner has a story.

✨

New Experiences

I believe life is too short to stick to a routine. Whether it's trying a new dish, visiting a new place, or picking up a new skill — I actively seek out experiences that broaden my perspective.

💻

Technology

Exploring how software, websites, and gadgets work fascinates me. I spend time reading tech news, watching tutorials, and experimenting with code in my free time.

🎵

Music

Music is my constant companion — Nepali folk, lo-fi beats, and everything in between. It keeps me focused during study sessions and energised during long days.

📚

Reading

I enjoy books on technology, science, and personal growth. Reading about the lives of great innovators inspires me to keep pushing my own boundaries and learning every day.

🔭

Lifelong Learning

Watching educational videos, exploring online courses, and picking up something new every week keeps my curiosity alive. Knowledge is the greatest thing one can collect.

"Passion is energy. Feel the power that comes from focusing on what excites you."
